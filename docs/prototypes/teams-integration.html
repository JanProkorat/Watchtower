Watchtower — Teams call button (Option A)

The Teams control is a standalone pill in the top-right corner of the title bar — separate from the instance tab group and out of the left rail. Click it to open the dedicated Teams window (calendar + join + call). The pill reflects state, and while you're on a call it's your way back to the call window from any scene.

Mechanism — a dedicated BrowserWindow loads teams.microsoft.com on a persistent session partition, so login sticks and nothing runs in the background once it's closed. The corner pill is the only always-present surface: click to open/focus, and it mirrors call state (open / On a call · timer). Closing the window (its red light) returns the pill to the resting state. Mic/cam/screen-share need setPermissionRequestHandler + setDisplayMediaRequestHandler. Presentation only.
