Watchtower iPad — Liquid Glass prototype

Every iPad surface in the approved frosted-floating glass language: frosted chrome floating over a lit ambient background, purple/cyan accents, glass cards and drawers. Dense data (ledgers, grids) keep a near-solid inner panel for legibility; charts sit on glass frames. Czech UI throughout. Presentation only — no behaviour change.
